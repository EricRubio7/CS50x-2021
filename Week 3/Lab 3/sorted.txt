Exercise: Identify what type of sorting method does each of the sort files use

sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: Runs O(n) in sorted case and at O(n^2) in unsorted case

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: Runs at O(n*logn) in both sorted and unsorted cases

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?: Runs at O(n^2) for both sorted and unsorted cases
